Target Markets

Families

  • See where your family members are at any point in time. Great for elders and children
  • Draw geographic zones on the map and get notified when your child enters and/or exits the zone
  • Get notified if your child's speed reaches a set threshold
  • Register your child as an "administered account" to have more control over their activities

Businesses

  • Automate labor hours by setting geographic zones on the map
  • Track your employees location and driving speed while they're out in the field. Get notified if speed reaches a set threshold
  • Register your employees as an "administered account" to have more control over their activities

Young Adults

  • Use the map to see which bar your friends are at in-case you get split-up
  • Shout a message anywhere on the map that your friends can see. Attach pictures and videos to share moments like Snapchat
  • Draw a home-base zone on the map to automatically notify your friends you made it home safe
